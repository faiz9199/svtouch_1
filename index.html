About Svtouch

Svtouch is a startup mentoring company founded by Nitin Bansal and John Carpinelli in 2012. However, the company was shut down in 2016 and then restarted in 2017 as an initiative from IIT Jobs Inc. The company is dedicated to helping entrepreneurs succeed by providing comprehensive services and mentorship.
The SV Touch Startup Mentoring Company is an innovative organization dedicated to helping entrepreneurs succeed. Our comprehensive services provide budding entrepreneurs with the necessary resources to launch their start-ups, including mentorship and guidance from experienced professionals
